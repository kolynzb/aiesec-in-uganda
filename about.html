About AIESEC

We are a youth leadership movement.

We are passionately driven by one cause, peace and fulfillment of humankind's potential.

AIESEC is a non-governmental not-for-profit organisation in consultative status with the United Nations Economic and Social Council (ECOSOC), affiliated with the UN DPI, member of ICMYO, and is recognized by UNESCO. AIESEC International Inc. is registered as a Not-for-profit Organisation under the Canadian Not-for-profit Corporations Act - 2018-02-08, Corporation Number: 1055154-6 and Quebec Business Number (NEQ) 1173457178 in Montreal, Quebec, Canada.

We develop youth leaders

We believe that youth are the key to shaping a better future. Through our experiences we strive to develop youth leaders with the following qualities.

Empowering Others

Engage with all kinds of people towards achieving a purpose bigger than yourself. This is how you can empower others around you

Solution Oriented

Volunteering abroad isn't easy, but surpassing challenges is what makes it worth it. You will learn how to be solution-oriented.

Self Awareness

Going beyond what you know opens up new worlds for you. Find your values, explore your passions, and become more self-aware.

World Citizen

Making your contribution and seeing your impact in real-time will show you that you can indeed make a difference the world.

FAQS

What is AIESEC?

AIESEC is a global platform for young people to explore and develop their leadership potential. We are a non-political, independent, not-for-profit organisation run by students and recent graduates of institutions of higher education. Its members are interested in world issues, leadership and management.

What is the goal of AIESEC?

AIESEC’s ultimate goal is develop youth leadership.

Why is it important to develop leadership?

The youth of today are the adults of tomorrow and they are going to be involved in every field in the society that’s why developing their leadership skills will make them active members in their societies and make the world a better place overall.

How do I sign up?

You can sign up by visiting aiesec.org and clicking on sign up. You can also sign up by contacting you local AIESEC office.

What kind of products does AIESEC offer?

AIESEC offers three exchange programs which are Global Volunteer, Global Talent and Global Teacher.

Where can I go on an opportunity with AIESEC?

AIESEC exists in more than 123 countries where you can go on an opportunity.
