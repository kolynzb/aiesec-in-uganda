About AIESEC in Uganda

Develop your leadership with aiesec.

AIESEC in Uganda is one of the oldest entities in AIESEC, established in 1948. Since then, we have grown into five local committees in four of the major cities in Uganda: Oslo, Bergen, Trondheim and Tromsø. Our mission is to help young people develop their leadership skills through professional and volunteer experiences, and through cultural exchange. We believe that sustainable, informed and globally-minded leadership can help us solve many issues faced by society today. Thus, we offer the youth of Uganda the opportunity to live and work abroad whilst contributing to one of the 17 sustainable development goals of the UN.

About us

AIESEC is a global platform for young people to develop their leadership potential through international internships and volunteer opportunities. Founded in 1948, AIESEC is a non-governmental, and not-for-profit organization entirely run by youth for youth.
