Hire young talent from around the world

AIESEC offers talent solutions to a variety of organizations such as companies, start-ups, and NGO's to find and bring on board the right candidate for the job from our pool of global talents.

Become a partner

Our Global Volunteer program for NGO's brings youth volunteers to social impact projects that contribute to the Sustainable Development Goals.

  • Duration: 6-8 Weeks.
  • Target: NGOs.
  • Benefits: Our network gives you instant access to young talent from over 120 countries and territories from all around the world.

Our Global Talent program helps companies fulfill their Human Resources needs with skilled youth ready to work in their field of expertise.

  • Duration:
    • 6-12 Weeks.
    • 13-26 Weeks.
    • 27-78 Weeks.
  • Target: Companies.
  • Benefits: Our network gives you instant access to young talent from over 120 countries and territories from all around the world.

Our Global Teacher program helps educational institutions or organizations fulfil their needs for teachers with skilled young people from all around the world.

  • Duration:
    • 9-12 Weeks.
    • 13-26 Weeks.
    • 27-78 Weeks.
  • Target: Educational institutions and Organizations.
  • Benefits: Our network gives you instant access to young talent from over 120 countries and territories from all around the world.

About us

AIESEC is a global platform for young people to develop their leadership potential through international internships and volunteer opportunities. Founded in 1948, AIESEC is a non-governmental, and not-for-profit organization entirely run by youth for youth.
